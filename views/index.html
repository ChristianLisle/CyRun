How To Play

  • PacMan: Collect resources to earn points. Grab a power pill to scare the ghosts and try to catch them to earn points.
  • Ghosts: Try to catch PacMan as many times as you can. Each time will earn you points.
  • When PacMan is caught, he will safely respawn at a random location on the board.

Controls

  • Desktop: Press the arrow keys to change the direction of your player.
  • Mobile: While your finger is on the game board, swipe in any direction to move your player.

Scoring

  • Who Wins: Either PacMan or the three ghosts will become victorious. The scores of the three ghosts are combined after the game to determine if PacMan or the ghosts scored the most points.
  • Scoring System
  • Pacman consumes a dot or pill - 1 point
  • Pacman consumes a ghost - 2 points
  • Ghost kills Pacman - 15 points

Play with Friends

  • Either join an existing lobby or start a new one by entering the lobby name.
  • Share the lobby name to play against friends.
